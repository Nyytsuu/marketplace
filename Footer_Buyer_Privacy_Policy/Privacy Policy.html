Privacy Policy

At CartIT, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, shipping address, phone number, payment details (only via secure processors).
  • Account Information: Username, password, order history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data (to improve our services).
  • Cookies: We use cookies to help remember you and provide a better experience. You can disable them in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process orders and payments
  • Contact you about your orders or account

3. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only with:

We use security tools like encryption, secure servers, and limited access to keep your data safe. While we do our best to protect your information, no systen can guarantee 100% security.
